Welcome to Green Valley School

Green Valley School is a place where learning becomes fun and meaningful. Our school was established in

2005 with the aim of providing quality education and building strong values in students. We believe that

every child has special talents, and our goal is to help students discover and develop those talents.

Green Valley School is more than just a place of learning. It is a family where students grow academically,

socially, and emotionally. We are proud of our students and their achievements.
